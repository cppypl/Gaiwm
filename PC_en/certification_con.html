Assistant Wealth Manager (AWM)

The Certified Financial Planner program provides entry-level basic equations for those interested in wealth management, covering the basic elements of financial planning, the value of practice, the measurement of benefits and risks, the asset allocation process, and the introduction of various financial instruments. Through the courses, students can have a deeper understanding of wealth management and the ability to preliminarily analysis programs. Each course module takes about 8-12 hours of study.
Through the wealth manager certification courses, students can have a better understanding of the source of wealth, master how to measure wealth, and understand various needs of risk and investment in the different life stages of clients, thus customizing reasonable investment plans for them. Each course module takes about 10-14 hours of study to get familiar with each knowledge point.

Certified Wealth Manager (CWM)

Learning to certify wealth management programs enables wealth management professionals to understand the source of wealth, master how to measure wealth, and understand the impact and different needs of customers at their stage of risk and investment. Learn how to judge the type and preference of individual investors through questionnaires. Understand the potential benefits of various investment products to investors and consultants. You should also know how to set up a portfolio based on your risk appetite and develop a reasonable investment plan for your clients. Master the knowledge and skills necessary for wealth management to provide tailored solutions for different clients.
Each course module requires about 10~14 hours of study. We will provide exercises to help the students familiarize themselves with each knowledge point. After the completion of the study, a unified standardized test will be conducted, and the certificate will be awarded by the Institute.

Family Wealth Legacy Planning Advisor

Through the courses, students can gain a solid grasp of investment banking, hedge funds, venture capital, financial analysis, financial modeling and other financial knowledge and have a profound understanding of the family office's service scope, operating model, wealth heritage planning and comprehensive capital management skills, etc. They can also provide customers with global investment, portfolio management, family office construction and other high-end financial services. At this stage, each course module takes about 15-20 hours to learn, so as to achieve full understanding and flexible application.
